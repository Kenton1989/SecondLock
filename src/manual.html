    Why named "SecondLock"?

    The name has multiple meaning

    1. "Second" refers to a time unit. The name shows the extension is used to "lock" your time for mindful usage.
    2. "Second" refers to "secondary". To truly save your time, your mind is the primary lock, this extension is just the secondary lock.
    3. "Second" refers to the second app. This extension is inspired by Mr. He's app - TimeLock. This extension is just "the second TimeLock".
